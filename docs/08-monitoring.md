# Monitoring and alerts

## Data monitoring
- Ingestion job success rate
- Completeness per league and market
- Staleness of last snapshot per fixture

## Model monitoring
- Drift in predicted probabilities by league
- Calibration drift (monthly)
- CLV trend and decay

## Execution monitoring
- Recommendation count by league and edge bucket
- Accepted vs rejected due to filters
- Stake distribution and exposure

## Alerts
- Data gaps for upcoming fixtures
- Sudden drop in CLV
- Parser failures and mapping mismatches
